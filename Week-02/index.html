About Us

Megarocket SAgym was founded in 1995 by a former Olympic athlete named Max Strong. Max had always been passionate about fitness and wanted to create a gym that would provide people with a welcoming and inclusive environment to pursue their fitness goals.

Over the years, Megarocket SA grew into one of the most popular gyms in the city, with a thriving community of members who were passionate about fitness. Max continued to run the gym until he retired in 2020.
